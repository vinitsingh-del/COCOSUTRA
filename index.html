Common Questions

Everything you should know before you buy

Is this really sugar-free? What sweetener is used?+
Yes, 100% sugar-free. We use Stevia and Erythritol, both naturally derived zero-calorie sweeteners with a low glycemic index. No aspartame, no sucralose — a clean, rich taste with no aftertaste.
Is it safe for diabetics?+
Absolutely. With just 2g net carbs and zero sugar per serving, it causes no blood sugar spike. Widely recommended by nutritionists and endocrinologists across India.
What is the difference between Classic and Functional?+
Classic flavours are pure gourmet drinking chocolates — rich, indulgent, versatile. Functional flavours add targeted superfoods such as Melatonin, Ashwagandha, or Prebiotics for specific wellness goals.
How do I prepare it? Can it be served cold?+
Mix 2 heaped teaspoons or one sachet with 150 to 200ml of hot milk or water. For iced chocolate, dissolve in a little hot water first, then add cold milk and ice. It works brilliantly both ways.
Is it keto-friendly?+
Yes. With just 2g net carbs per serving, it fits comfortably within a ketogenic diet. No hidden sugars, no maltodextrin, no fillers.
Which product should I start with?+
We recommend the 6-Flavour Sampler for first-time buyers. If wellness is your priority, start with the Functional Wellness Sampler covering all four functional variants in single-serve sachets.
